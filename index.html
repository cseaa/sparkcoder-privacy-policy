SparkCoder Policy

Privacy Policy

Last modified: January 2, 2025

At SparkCoder, we build educational tools with a strong commitment to privacy. When you use our services, you trust us with your information. We understand this is a big responsibility and work hard to protect your information and put you in control.

This Privacy Policy is meant to help you understand what information we collect, why we collect it, and how you can update, manage, and delete your information.

1. Child Safety & Compliance

Commitment to Kids' Safety

SparkCoder is designed for children. We adhere strictly to the Google Play Families Policy, COPPA (Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act), and GDPR standards regarding children's data.

We adhere to a strict "No-Tracking" policy for child users. We do not engage in behavioral advertising, and we do not collect precise location data from children.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to all our users—from figuring out which level you are on, to delivering your physical certificates.

A. Information You Create or Provide to Us

  • Local Profile Data: When a child sets up a profile (e.g., "Nickname" or "Age Group"), this data is stored locally on your device via localStorage. It is not automatically uploaded to our servers unless a parent specifically initiates a cloud sync or backup.
  • Parent Information: To access the "Parents Zone" or purchase premium plans, parents may provide an email address or phone number for authentication and receipt generation.
  • Shipping Information: If you purchase a plan that includes physical goods (Hard Copy Certificates, Medals, or Kits), we collect your Shipping Address solely for delivery purposes.

B. Information Collected Automatically

  • App Progress: We store game progress (XP, Levels Unlocked, Badges) locally on the device to ensure the user can continue where they left off.
  • Device Information: We may collect non-personal device information (e.g., device model, operating system version) to ensure the app runs smoothly and to debug crashes.

3. How We Use Data

We use the data we collect for the following specific purposes:

  • To maintain & improve our services: Ensuring the app remembers your child's learning progress.
  • To fulfill orders: Using parent-provided addresses to ship physical rewards (Medals/Certificates).
  • To communicate: Sending payment receipts or support updates to the parent's email.

4. Third-Party Services

We partner with trusted third-party processors to handle specific functions. These partners are strictly prohibited from using your data for any other purpose.

  • Razorpay: We use Razorpay for secure payment processing. SparkCoder does not store your credit card or banking details on our servers. All financial transactions are handled directly by Razorpay's encrypted infrastructure.
  • Google Play Services: Used for app distribution and core functionality on Android devices.

5. Data Security

We build security into our services to protect your information. We use encryption (HTTPS/SSL) to keep your data private while in transit. Access to personal information (such as shipping addresses) is restricted to SparkCoder employees and contractors who need that information in order to process it.

6. Your Controls & Choices

You have choices regarding the information we collect and how it's used.

  • Access & Update: You can update your profile information directly within the app settings.
  • Delete Data: Parents can request the complete deletion of their account and associated data by contacting our support team.
  • Local Data: You can clear the app's local storage or uninstall the app to remove locally stored progress.
