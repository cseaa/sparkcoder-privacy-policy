Privacy Policy - SparkCoder

Last Updated: January 2025

SparkCoder ("we", "our", "us") is an educational coding platform designed especially for kids. We respect your privacy and are fully committed to protecting personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how our app handles user data and ensures child safety.

Kids Safety First

SparkCoder is built for children. We strictly follow:
✔ Google Play Families Policy
✔ COPPA (Children Online Privacy Protection Rule)
✔ GDPR Requirements

Information We Collect

SparkCoder does NOT collect any unnecessary or harmful data. We do NOT collect:

❌ No Personal Identification Data ❌ No Contact Details ❌ No Photos / Camera Data ❌ No Microphone Recording ❌ No Location Tracking ❌ No Sensitive Information

The only basic information stored on the device may include:

  • Kid Name (for display only)
  • Age Group Selection (for learning level customization)
  • XP, Learning Progress & Game Levels

This data never leaves the device.

Parent Zone & Purchases

SparkCoder includes a Parent Zone protected by verification. Parents may optionally provide:

  • Parent Name
  • Contact Number / Email
  • Delivery Address (ONLY if purchasing Physical Kit / Medal / Certificate Shipping)

This is collected ONLY to deliver:

  • Physical Coding Kit
  • Hard Copy Certificate
  • Medal / Rewards

We do NOT sell or share parent information with any third party.

Payments

Payments are handled securely using Razorpay. We do NOT store any card/bank/payment information. All transactions are handled securely by Razorpay under RBI-compliant encryption.

Ads Policy

SparkCoder does NOT show inappropriate, harmful or unsafe ads. No behavioral tracking is done on children.

Data Sharing

We DO NOT share, sell, trade or rent user data. We DO NOT share data with advertisers or analytics companies.

Data Security

We apply strong measures to protect information. Any stored learning data remains safe.

Parental Control Rights

Parents can:

  • Request deletion of any stored optional information
  • Request details of stored data
  • Contact us anytime
